## Abstract Submission

**Deadline: Friday, February 6, 2026**

Abstracts must include the following information:

- Presentation title
- Co-authors and affiliations
- Abstract (200 word max)
- 3 keywords
- Session topic (rank top 3)

### Register First

All presenters must register for the conference before they can submit an abstract. After registration is processed, you will receive an email from no-reply@oxfordabstracts.com with the subject line *"You're registered for 54th Benthic Ecology Meeting Society"* containing a link to submit your abstract.

![Abstract Submission Screenshot](/images/Pic_SubmitAbstract.png)

If you don't see the email within 24 hours, check your spam folder before contacting [bem2026@vims.edu](mailto:bem2026@vims.edu).
