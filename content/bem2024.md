## Welcome to the 52nd Annual Benthic Ecology Meeting

**When:** April 10–14, 2024  
**Where:** Charleston, South Carolina (USA)  
**Venue:** Charleston Marriott

### Welcome!

The 52nd Annual Benthic Ecology Meeting was held in Charleston, South Carolina (USA) from April 10–14, 2024 at the Charleston Marriott. The hotel is located next to the Ashley River on the historic and vibrant downtown Charleston peninsula.

## Conference Venue

Charleston Marriott  
170 Lockwood Dr, Charleston, SC 29403  
[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/txhTLDWUa9Q8gS8T6)

## Conference Banquet

The traditional end-of-conference banquet was at the [SC Aquarium](https://scaquarium.org) on the Cooper River. The banquet included food, drinks, and full access to the Aquarium and sweeping views of Charleston Harbor.
