## FAQs

#### What should I budget for?

Registration ranges from $250–$450. The Marriott and Hilton charge for parking (max $30/day) — hourly rates are on the [Travel and Accommodations](/meeting-2026/travel/) page. Field trips and workshops on Sunday March 1 are listed on the [Optional Programs](/meeting-2026/fieldtrips/) page. The banquet on Wednesday March 4 is $25.

#### Do I need to create an account on Oxford Abstracts?

Yes. Create an account with your email and a password. Remember your login so you can return to view your registration at a later date.

#### Can I register now and come back later to add field trips or a banquet ticket?

Yes. After completing registration, a button to purchase add-ons will appear in the bottom right corner. You can also access add-ons later via the "View Your Order" button in your confirmation email.

#### Can I pay for registration separately from field trips and the banquet?

Yes. Complete the form and select the items you want to purchase together. After the first purchase, a button to purchase add-ons appears in the bottom right corner, where you can specify a different payment method.

#### What are the payment options?

You may pay by card via the registration portal. If you require an invoice, email a request to [BEM2026@vims.edu](mailto:BEM2026@vims.edu). Once your invoice is paid, you will receive a code to register through the portal.

#### Can I register multiple attendees at once?

Yes. In Step 1: Select Tickets, hit the plus button to add tickets. In Step 2, a button next to each ticket lets you indicate whether it is for yourself or a different attendee. If for a different attendee, you will be prompted to enter their email.

#### How do I submit an abstract?

You will receive a link to the abstract submission form in your registration confirmation email.

#### Do I qualify to register as a student?

The student rate is intended to offset costs for undergraduate and graduate students. Post-docs should register as professionals.

#### Should I register as a presenter?

Only the primary presenter should indicate "yes" on the form. Co-authors who are not the primary presenter should answer "no."

#### I can no longer attend. What is the cancellation policy?

Email [bem2026@vims.edu](mailto:bem2026@vims.edu) to cancel. Cancellation requests received by **February 1, 2026** will be refunded in full minus a $50 service charge. No refunds will be issued after February 1, 2026. Refunds will not be issued to no-shows. BEM is not responsible for hotel arrangements or cancellations.
