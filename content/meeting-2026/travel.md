## Venue

The official hotel and venue for BEM 2026 is the [Marriott Virginia Beach Oceanfront Resort](https://www.cavalierresortvb.com/marriott-virginia-beach-oceanfront-resort).

## Accommodations

Room blocks are available at the [Marriott hotel](https://www.marriott.com/event-reservations/reservation-link.mi?id=1738787810995&key=GRP&guestreslink2=true&app=resvlink) and the adjacent [Embassy Suites by Hilton](https://www.hilton.com/en/attend-my-event/orfanes-abm-93230f38-ddc2-4541-950a-64505a34e89f/). Both hotels are part of the Cavalier resort on the north end of the Virginia Beach boardwalk overlooking the Atlantic Ocean. We have arranged a discounted room rate of **$124 per room per night** plus taxes (16.5%) and a $2/night occupancy fee. The room rate expires on 2/1/2026.

When you book online, a resort fee ($30/day) is added, but **the resort fee will be waived upon arrival**. Your stay includes access to resort activities (indoor pool, gym, bike rentals, etc).

- **Marriott:** Rate is the same regardless of number of occupants.
- **Embassy Suites:** Includes a made-to-order breakfast buffet and 2 drinks at the Executive Happy Hour. Rate applies for 2 guests per room; 3+ guests incur a $35 per person charge.

During check-in, guests will need to provide a method of payment (preferably credit or debit). If you need to charge the hotel directly to a grant/index without a card, contact the hotel ahead of time and sign a Certify Form prior to arrival.

Guests must sign an authorization for incidentals ($100/night at Marriott, $150/night at Embassy Suites). If no incidentals are incurred, funds will be reimbursed after checkout.
