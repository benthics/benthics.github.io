## Registration Information

Everyone must register for the conference, regardless of whether they are a presenter or not. Early-bird registration is due January 16, 2026 and abstract submissions are due February 6, 2026.

[Register Now!](https://app.oxfordabstracts.com/register/event/75374)

### Registration Fees

| Category | Early-Bird (ends Jan 16) | Regular |
|----------|--------------------------|---------|
| Professional (faculty, postdocs, staff, etc.) | $400 | $450 |
| Student | $250 | $300 |
| Daily or Guest (per day entry) | $250 | $250 |

Questions about the Oxford Abstracts system and payment options? Please refer to our [FAQ](/meeting-2026/faq/) page.

### Cancellation Policies

#### Registration Cancellation

Should you need to cancel your participation in the BEM 2026 conference, you must email us at [bem2026@vims.edu](mailto:bem2026@vims.edu).

- Cancellation requests received by **February 1, 2026** will be refunded in full minus a $50 service charge.
- No refunds will be issued after **February 1, 2026**.
- Refunds will not be issued to no-shows.

#### Banquet Cancellation

Should you need to cancel your banquet ticket, email [bem2026@vims.edu](mailto:bem2026@vims.edu) by February 1, 2026 for a refund, minus a $50 service charge.

#### Lodging Cancellation

See hotel policies. BEM is not responsible for hotel arrangements or cancellations.
