#### Online Poster Gallery

Oxford Abstracts allows poster presenters to share an electronic version of their poster in the online poster gallery, which includes a chat function for connecting with other attendees.

- [Help: the Poster Gallery](https://help.oxfordabstracts.com/knowledge/exploring-the-poster-gallery)

To participate, go to your [Oxford Abstracts dashboard](https://app.oxfordabstracts.com/stages/78703/submitter), click the red "incomplete" button, and upload your file.

**Please note:** All presenters must print their poster and bring a physical copy to the conference. Participation in the online poster gallery does not replace the requirement for in-person participation.

### Hotel Reminders

Hotel check-in begins at 4:00 PM. Luggage storage is available in the Kaye room (second floor) for early arrivals.

During check-in, provide a method of payment (preferably credit or debit). If you need to charge directly to a grant/index, contact the hotel ahead of time to sign a Certify Form. Guests must sign an authorization for incidentals ($100/night at Marriott, $150/night at Embassy Suites) — funds are reimbursed after checkout if no incidentals are incurred.

See the [Travel and Accommodations](/meeting-2026/travel/) page for further details.
